About Me


I am Mosa - a very positive, free spirited person who loves being around and meeting new people. A daughter, sister and an aunty - I enjoy spending my free time around loved ones. For fun I like going out for drinks, going to the cinema or being outdoors - I'm a very adventurous person! I love socializing with others and playing games - though I can be quite competitive!

I am a team player who always looks out for the best interests of her team. I enjoy being challenged and I’ve got a ‘never going to give up’ mind set. When working on a project, I am goal driven and time conscious. I enjoy learning new things, no matter how hard they appear to be initially. I am very understanding and I am a good listener. I’m the type of person that looks for solutions instead of complaining. A hard worker, who is good at multitasking and easy to work with.

I am an ADA graduate!

The journey at ADA was amazing and I got to learn a lot more about myself. It was not just a school - it became a second home where you could be yourself and be free to make mistakes without being judged. My peers and lecturers made it easy for you to be open about anything whether it was about school or home. Showed that if you want something you should do it and believe that. ADA has showed that I am a strong person who is unique for a reason.


Starting at ADA was never the hardest part - pushing through and always believing the school made the right decision by choosing me to be part of the experience was! The lecturers always told us we were the best, which always motivated me because I knew I was amongst the 2.2% of the people that were hand picked to participate. I never felt stupid being there - it weas always a fun environment to learn new front-end development skills. Sadly covid shortened the experience for me and only hoped that ADA was a stepping stone on the new career path I've chosen. I'm very grateful for the opportunities I got to learn.
